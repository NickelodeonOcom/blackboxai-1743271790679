Frequently Asked Questions

How do I choose the right paddle?

Consider your playing style - offensive players may prefer faster blades with spinny rubbers, while defensive players might want more control.

What's the difference between 1-star and 3-star balls?

3-star balls are ITTF approved for competition with better consistency and durability, while 1-star balls are better for casual play.

How often should I replace my rubbers?

Competitive players typically replace rubbers every 3-6 months, while recreational players can go 6-12 months depending on usage.
